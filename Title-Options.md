## Leading with the horse
(the Galloping Horse is the centerpiece image of the essay)

- *Alone with the Galloping Horse*

## Leading with place and moment

- *When China Came to Kansas City*
- *The Night Shift at the Nelson Gallery*

## More atmospheric

- *Brushes, Bells, and Ancient Bronze*
- *After Hours in Ancient China*

## Notes

**"Alone with the Galloping Horse"** — strongest candidate. Specific, evocative,
and captures the most unique part of the story: the private encounter with the
centerpiece artifact that daytime visitors could never really have.

**"When China Came to Kansas City"** — good if the title should signal the broader
historical scope before narrowing into the personal.
